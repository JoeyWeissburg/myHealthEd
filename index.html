What is MyHealthEd?

MyHealthEd is a tailored online sexual health learning tool for middle and high school students in rural North Carolina. Although required by North Carolina law, many students do not receive a comprehensive sexual health education. This leads to a higher rate of teen pregnancy and sexually transmitted infections in rural parts of North Carolina. The purpose of MyHealthEd is to provide an accessible source of sexual health information online that is tailored for each student. A prototype is planned to pilot in five rural public schools in spring 2014.

The project is currently being developed on three fronts: the curriculum, the online tool, and the research to evaluate its effectiveness. This Github page and associated code repository is home base for the online software. The primary goals are to create web-based activities based on the North Carolina standards in sexual education. The scope of portion developed here may grow throughout through the second half of the year.

The content presented on this site is concerned primarily with the online activies and web tools. To learn more about MyHealthEd, visit the microryza page.
